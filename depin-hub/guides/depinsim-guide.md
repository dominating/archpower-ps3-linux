# Depinsim (ESIM) Setup Guide

**Depinsim (ESIM)** is a decentralized telecom frontier DePIN focused on reshaping connectivity. Launched alongside KuCoin premieres, it is positioning itself for a major Q1/Q2 2026 expansion.

## Requirements
- Mobile device (eSIM compatible preferred).
- Compatible crypto wallet.

## Setup Instructions

1. **Visit the Official Portal**: Head to the official Depinsim platform or app.
2. **Register**: Create an account and link your Web3 wallet.
3. **Activate eSIM / Node**: Follow the in-app instructions to provision your decentralized eSIM profile or node connection.
4. **Earn ESIM**: Stay connected. Your device acts as a decentralized telecom node, earning ESIM tokens for network participation.

*Pro Tip: ESIMs make it easy to run this DePIN passively on your primary phone alongside your usual cellular plan!*
