# Kaisar Network (KAI) Guide

**Kaisar Network** is a DePIN project optimized for AI and compute, built on the peaq blockchain. It aggregates underutilized GPU power and offers Checker Nodes to secure the network.

## Requirements
- PC or VPS with supported hardware (idle GPU resources are prioritized).

## Setup Instructions

1. **Sign Up**: Visit [Kaisar Network](https://kaisar.io/) to join the early access / node runner waitlist.
2. **Set up Peaq Wallet**: Since Kaisar operates on the peaq network, you will need a compatible wallet (like SubWallet or Talisman).
3. **Run the Node**: Download the Kaisar Checker Node client from the official dashboard.
4. **Link Wallet**: Bind your peaq address to receive KAI testnet/mainnet rewards.
5. **Monitor Uptime**: Keep your device online to provide computing power and earn airdrop points.
