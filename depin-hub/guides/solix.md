# Solix Network

## Overview
Solix is a DePIN project transforming the internet with decentralized power. Launched in 2025, Solix democratizes internet infrastructure by allowing anyone with a stable internet connection to share their unused bandwidth and earn rewards.

## Key Features
*   **AI-Backed Ecosystem:** Solix integrates AI capabilities to optimize network routing and bandwidth allocation.
*   **Decentralized Infrastructure:** Helps build a peer-to-peer network, shifting control away from corporate data centers.
*   **Reward System:** Generates steady passive crypto rewards for contributors who maintain uptime.

## How to Get Started
1. Visit the official Solix portal to register.
2. Download and install the Solix node or browser extension.
3. Keep your node online to build reputation and increase rewards.
