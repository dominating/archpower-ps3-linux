# DAWN Network ($DAWN)

**DAWN** is a Solana-based DePIN project that turns your unused internet bandwidth into rewards. By participating in the network, you help build a decentralized and community-driven internet infrastructure.

## How to Earn

1. **Install the DAWN Validator Extension:** Download the Chrome extension and create an account.
2. **Run the Extension:** Keep the extension active in your browser while you surf the web. The extension validates your bandwidth and rewards you with $DAWN tokens.
3. **DAWN Black Box (Optional):** For higher rewards, you can purchase the DAWN Black Box hardware to provide dedicated, uninterrupted bandwidth to the network.

## Why DAWN?

- **Solana Ecosystem:** Fast, low-fee transactions and high scalability.
- **Passive Income:** Earn $DAWN effortlessly just by leaving the extension running.
- **Decentralized Internet:** Contribute to a more robust, distributed web infrastructure instead of relying on centralized corporate ISPs.
