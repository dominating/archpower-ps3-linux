# Orochi Network (ONProver Node) Guide

Orochi Network is a $12M-backed verifiable compute protocol launching on the Monad ecosystem. By running an **ONProver** node, you help process and verify computations on the Monad Testnet, earning points toward the upcoming $ON token airdrop.

## Requirements
* A Web3 Wallet (e.g., MetaMask, Rabby)
* Monad Testnet configured in your wallet
* Monad Testnet tokens (MON) for gas fees

## Step-by-Step Setup

1. **Connect & Register:** Navigate to the [Orochi Network ONProver Portal](https://orochi.network/).
2. **Complete Captcha & Sign Up:** Create an ONID account using your email or wallet.
3. **Connect Wallet:** Link your Web3 wallet and ensure you are connected to the **Monad Testnet**.
4. **Start the Prover:** On your dashboard, locate the **"Prover"** or **"Run Node"** button. Click it to initialize the lightweight node directly from your device.
5. **Keep it Running:** Leave the tab/process open to actively verify network transactions and accumulate airdrop points before the TGE.
